Reaspberry Smoothie Drink

Desciption

The recipe is pretty easy for this one. All you need a blender and the Ingredients given below.
Just blend all the ingredients together until they are smooth. That's it.

Ingredients

  • 1 small banana
  • 1/2 cup blackberries
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 4 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place Banana, blackberries, raspberries, honey, yogurt and ice cubes into a blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Enjoy!
