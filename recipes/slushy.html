Watermelon Cooler Slushy

Desciption

This refreshing drink will keep you cool in the summer. You get to enjoy watermelon without having to
stiff throught watermelon seeds.

Ingredients

  • 4 cup seedless cube watermelons
  • 10 ice cubes
  • 1/3 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1/4 cup of white suger
  • 1/8 tablespoon salt

Steps

  1. Place watermelon and ice into the blender
  2. Pour in lime juice, suger and salt in there
  3. Blend until smooth
