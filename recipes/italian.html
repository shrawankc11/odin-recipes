Italian Stuffed Zucchini

Desciption

The recipe requires you to have a oven, pasta sauce, Parmasen cheese and zucchini
Choose the best zucchini out there to make this recipe delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 Zucchini
  • 2 tablespoon italian-style bread crumbs
  • 2 tables spoon grated Parmasen cheese
  • 2/3 cup frozen burger-style crumbles
  • 1/2 cup spaghetti sauce
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Prehead the oven to 350 degress F
  2. Slice each zucchini in half lengthwise
  3. Hollo out the zucchini
  4. combine scraped zucchini, bread crumbs, parmasen cheese, burger-style crumbles, pasta sauce
  5. Mix well
  6. Place mixture in hollowed out zucchini
  7. Arrange zucchini, sprinkle mozzarella cheese
  8. Bake for 40 minutes
